Course Overview - Mastering IT Foundations

Welcome to the IT Foundations course! This is your first step into the exciting world of computers and technology.

What is IT Foundations?

This course teaches you the basics of how computers work, how to keep them safe, and how to solve simple computer problems.

What Will You Learn?

  • Basics of Computer Technology
  • How Computer Hardware Works
  • Understanding Computer Networks
  • Different Types of Computer Systems
  • How to Stay Safe Online
  • Cool Future Technology

Why Take This Course?

Learning about technology can help you understand how computers work and maybe even help you start a cool tech career in the future!

What You'll Be Able to Do

  • Understand how computers and technology work
  • Fix simple computer problems
  • Learn how to keep your computer and information safe
  • Get ready for future technology classes
